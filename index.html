About

Step into the heartwarming world of Kopi Kita, a charming cafe lovingly crafted by three young and passionate Kelantanese individuals: Asmaa Atikah, Nursolehah, and Fadhilah. Nestled in the heart of the city, Kopi Kita embodies the essence of Kelantanese culture and hospitality. With a dedication to excellence, they serve up an array of tantalizing coffee blends, each crafted with precision and care. Their menu boasts a fusion of traditional Kelantanese delicacies and modern twists, ensuring every bite is a delightful journey. From rich pasta dishes to mouthwatering pastries, Kopi Kita promises an unforgettable experience, where flavors dance and friendships flourish. Come, join the trio in their cozy corner, and savor the taste of Kelantan at its finest.
